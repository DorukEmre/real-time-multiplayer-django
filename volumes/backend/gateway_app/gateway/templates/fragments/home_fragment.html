Welcome to Methane Hunters

Exploring the cosmos to tackle the methane challenge.

The Methane Crisis

Cows' methane emissions are a significant global issue, as methane is 25 times more effective than CO₂ at trapping heat, contributing heavily to global warming.

Our Mission

At Methane Hunters, we combine agriculture and space technology, using satellites and solar-powered bioreactors to reduce methane emissions.

Join us as we embark on a mission to use technology, space exploration, and renewable energy to solve one of Earth’s biggest environmental challenges.
