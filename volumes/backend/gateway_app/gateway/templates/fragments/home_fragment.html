The Methane Crisis

Cows may roam the Earth, but their methane emissions are a global problem reaching the skies. Methane is a potent greenhouse gas, and livestock farming is a major contributor. It is 25 times more effective than CO₂ at trapping heat in the atmosphere, making it a major driver of global warming.

Our Mission

At Methane Hunters, we explore the intersection of agriculture and space to innovate new ways of managing methane emissions. From satellite monitoring to advanced bioreactors powered by the Sun, we aim to reduce methane’s footprint on our planet.

Join us as we embark on a mission to use technology, space exploration, and renewable energy to solve one of Earth’s biggest environmental challenges.
